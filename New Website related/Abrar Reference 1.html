Precaustions to take when there is a disaster

Disasters are very deadly and can kill you or injure you. For this, we have to prepare before a disaster. Some examples of precautions you should take are

 

-Store enough food and water in case of an immediate lockdown


-Always keep some money for emergencies


-Always keep emergency contacts on your phone contacts and keep a list of emergency contact numbers beside a home phone if you have one for kids at home to use if parents aren�脌 home


-Always have an evacuation plan at your house in case of emergency evacuation


-Always keep a first aid kit at your home and make sure everyone knows where it is


-Make sure your mode of transportation is in good condition for evacuation


-Ensure you know how to turn the gas, electricity, and water off and on in case of an accident


-Learn how to use a fire extinguisher and disaster kits


-Ensure your evacuation route and meetup
